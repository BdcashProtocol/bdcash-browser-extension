How it works

Connect to BDCash Ecosystem directly from your browser

1. Activate the plugin

You'll find it in the upper right-hand corner of your browser. One click and it's active.

2. Create new identity

Write your name or a cool nickname. A strong password is recommended.

3. That's it!

You already have your account. Now you can access the dApps from here.

dApps

Interact with the BDCash Ecosystem! (beta version).

BDCash Manent

An application that you can use as a Wallet in Cloud, which will allow you to send and recive LYRA. Backup wallet from here.

BDCash ID

With this service you'll be able to link one or more third party identities to your Lyra address. The identity payload will be encrypted with the same password of your address. You must save the updated version on your phone or on your PC downloading again the .sid file. No one can access your identity payloads, so save them in a safe place. To start select a .sid file from your system or enter using the BDCashID extension.

BDCash News

A webapp will allow publishers to upload their articles directly to the blockchain permanently, with e-signature and #timestamp. So it will be possible to be sure of the source origin and an editor will be able to protect his own works
